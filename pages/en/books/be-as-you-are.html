About This Book

Be As You Are is considered one of the most essential collections of Sri Ramana Maharshi's teachings. Edited by David Godman, it contains the core teachings on Self-enquiry, silence, and liberation.

Key Topics

  • Who am I? - Self-Enquiry Method
  • The Nature of the Self
  • The Mind and Its Modifications
  • Silence and Grace
  • Surrender to the Guru
  • Self-Realization and Liberation
