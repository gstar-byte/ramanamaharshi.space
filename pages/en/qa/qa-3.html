💬 Silence and Teaching

Why the Maharshi taught in silence and the power of silent presence.

❓ Why is silence the highest teaching?

Words are only pointers to the Truth. Truth itself is beyond words. Silence is the perfect instruction because it directly transmits the state of the Self.

❓ How does silent presence work?

In the presence of a Sage, the mind of the seeker naturally becomes still. This is the power of Grace acting through silence.

❓ Did the Maharshi ever talk?

He answered questions with great patience for those who could not understand his silence. But he always said that his silent teaching was more potent.
