💬 Practice of Self-Inquiry

Practical guidance on how to perform Atma-Vichara in daily life.

❓ How to start Self-Inquiry?

Whenever a thought arises, ask: 'To whom has this thought arisen?'. The answer will be 'To me'. Then ask: 'Who am I?'. This brings the mind back to its source.

❓ Can I practice while working?

Yes. Let the body do the work while the mind remains anchored in the Self. It is like an actor playing a role while knowing his true identity.

❓ What if I can't concentrate?

Concentration is not necessary. What is required is to turn the attention inward. Every time the mind wanders, bring it back by asking 'To whom did this thought occur?'.

❓ Is a Guru necessary?

The Guru is the Self. Externally, the Guru pushes you inward; internally, the Self pulls you in. They are one and the same.
