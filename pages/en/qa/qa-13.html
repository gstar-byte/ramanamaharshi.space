💬 Diet and Health

The impact of food on the mind and practice.

❓ What is the essence of Diet and Health?

In the Maharshi's teaching, diet and health is fundamentally about returning to the source. It is not about gaining something new, but about removing the ignorance that veils the truth.

❓ How should a seeker approach Diet and Health?

By remaining as one is, without the interference of the ego. This is the simplest and most direct way to realize the truth of any spiritual concept.
