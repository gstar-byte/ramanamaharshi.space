💬 Self and Ego

Fundamental questions on the difference between the true Self and the false ego.

❓ What is the Self?

The Self is the 'I am' - the pure consciousness that is the witness of all experience. It is not within time or space, nor does it depend on any external conditions. It is your deepest essence.

❓ What is the difference between Self and ego?

The Self is eternal existence, pure 'I am'. The ego is a false identification with the body, name, and role. Ego is a product of ignorance; the Self is Reality itself.

❓ How to recognize the Self?

By inquiring 'Who am I?'. When other thoughts arise, let them go and keep asking. This redirects attention inward until only the pure 'I am' remains.

❓ Where is the Self located in the body?

The Maharshi pointed to the 'Heart' (Hridaya) on the right side of the chest. This is not the physical heart but the spiritual center from which the 'I' arises.

❓ Is the mind an obstacle?

The mind is the only obstacle. It is nothing but a bundle of thoughts. When the mind is quieted through inquiry, the Self reveals itself spontaneously.
