💎 Essential Concepts

🔮 The Self / Atman

The eternal, unchanging reality that is the true nature of every being. Realizing Atman is the goal of human life.

🧠 The Mind

A collection of thoughts. Maharshi taught that the mind must be turned inward to find its source in the Self.

🔍 "Who am I?"

The most direct path to Self-Realization, where one traces the source of the "I" thought back to the Heart.

🤫 Silence

Considered by Maharshi as the highest and most potent form of teaching, transcending all spoken words.

✨ Grace / Anugraha

The divine power that aids the seeker. Maharshi taught that Guru, Grace, and Self are one and the same.

🙏 Surrender

The process of handing over the individual ego to the Divine. It is functionally identical to Self-Inquiry.
