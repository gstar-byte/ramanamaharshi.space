👤 About Maharshi

Sri Ramana Maharshi (1879-1950) is one of the greatest spiritual masters of modern India. At age 16, he had a spontaneous Self-realization experience. For the next fifty years, he lived in silence on Arunachala Hill, teaching the direct path of Self-inquiry "Who am I?"
